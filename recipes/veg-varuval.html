Vegetable Varuval Recipe

Description

A quick and healthy yet flavourful vegetable dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 5 small red onions
  • A handful of curry leaves
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 large potato, diced
  • 10 okra
  • 2 round brinjals
  • 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Wash okra. Cut off both ends and chop into 1 cm pieces.
  2. Stir fry onion and curry leaves on medium heat until soft.
  3. Add cumin powder, turmeric powder and diced potatoes.
  4. Mix well and stir-fry until potatoes are cooked.
  5. Add the other vegetables.
  6. Add curry powder and salt. Mix well.
  7. Keep stir-frying until cooked. Serve hot.
