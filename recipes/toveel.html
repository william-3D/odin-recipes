Mint Coriander Toveel (or Thogayal) Recipe

Description

A traditional South Indian condiment that puts your bottle of Tobasco to shame. Commonly eaten with rice and accompanying South Indian dishes.

Ingredients

  • A handful or 10 pieces of curry leaves
  • 2 garlic gloves
  • 2 small red onions
  • 2 large green chilies
  • 1/2 inch of ginger
  • A handful of mint leaves
  • A handful of coriander leaves
  • 3/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tsp salt
