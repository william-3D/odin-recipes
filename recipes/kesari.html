Kesari Recipe

Description

Never worry about what to bring to a friend's house ever again! Making kesari is far easier than baking a cake, and probably yields yummier results.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • A handful of cashew nuts, chopped
  • A handful of raisins
  • 1 1/4 cup roasted semolina flour
  • 2 cups water, boiling
  • 1.5 cups full cream milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 drops yellow food colouring

Steps

  1. Stir-fry cashew nuts and raisins in 1 tbsp ghee over medium-low heat.
  2. Once browned, add boiling water and full cream milk.
  3. Add salt, sugar and yellow food colouring.
  4. Bring mixture to a boil, then lower heat.
  5. Slowly add semolina flour, stirring as you add it to avoid lumps.
  6. Add the remaining 3 tbsp of ghee in.
  7. Stir vigorously until smooth.
  8. Spread some ghee on the inside of a baking tray.
  9. Ladle the kesari mixture into the baking tray.
  10. Spread to the corners and smoothen.
  11. Once cooled, cut into diamonds and serve.
